About ETC

Everything Type Company (ETC) is a NYC-based graphic design studio founded by Kyle Blue and Geoff Halber. We specialize in branding, art direction, publications, signage, and interactive design and development for clients spanning culture and commerce. Through a collaborative process with our clients, our work materializes from a distinct visual direction and a sensibility for production processes and technology. We are an agile studio committed to creating unique, well-crafted design solutions.
